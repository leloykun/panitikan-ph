How my Brother Leon Brought Home a Wife

ni Manuel Arguilla

   Mapapansin sa akdang ito ang pagkadalubhasa ni Miguel Arguilla sa Ingles. Di tulad sa ibang mga akda sa Ingles na inilathala ilang dekada palang nakakaraan, ang akdang ito ay hindi “bland” at “generic” kundi mabuhay at detalyado. Ang bawat karakter ay makapani-paniwala at kawili-wili dahil na rin sa mga maliliit na detalye tulad ng dimples ni Maria, ang kanyang paraan pag-tawag kay Leon na “Noel”, kahit ang kalabaw, pinangalanan pa. Mararamdaman rin ang pagkapilipino sa akdang ito lalo na sa paglalarawan ng buhay probinsya at ng kapaligiran - para tayong dinala ni Miguel Arguilla sa bayang-tinubuan nya.

   The sun was in our eyes, for it was dipping into the bright sea. The sky was wide and deep and very blue above us: but along the saw-tooth rim of the Katayaghan hills to the southwest flamed huge masses of clouds. Before us the fields swam in a golden haze through which floated big purple and red and yellow bubbles when I looked at the sinking sun. Labang's white coat, which I had wshed and brushed that morning with coconut husk, glistened like beaten cotton under the lamplight and his horns appeared tipped with fire.

   Bagaman ay si Baldo ang unang persona, ang maikling kuwento ay tungkol sa mga pagsubok ni Maria, isang taga-lunsod, kung makakaya niyang tumira sa probinsya at maging kapwa ng pamilya ng mahal niyang si Leon. Di natinag ang loob ni Maria at ginawa pang di-makakalimutang ala-ala ang sitwasyun.

   All the laughter seemed to have gone out of her. I waited for my brother Leon to say something, but he was not saying anything. Suddenly he broke out into song and the song was 'Sky Sown with Stars'---the same that he and Father sang when we cut hay in the fields at night before he went away to study. He must have taught her the song because she joined him, and her voice flowed into his like a gentle stream meeting a stronger one.

   Si Arguilla ay nagmula rin sa simpleng pamilya kaya marahil na gusto niyang ipahiwatig na hindi hadlang sa tunay na pagmamahal - isang konseptong mas natanggap dahil maganda talagang pambalanse ang libreng edukasyon na binigay satin ng mga Amerikano.
